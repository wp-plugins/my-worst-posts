== Description ==
a Widget Show the Worst Posts in your blog - the less comments - , because may be some one get interested and decide to comment :)

This Plugin Coded by : Waseem Senjer , for any suggestions , you can email me at waseem.senjer@gmail.com

== Installation ==



1. Upload `worst-posts` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. In your "Widget" menu add the widget to your sidebar
1. enjoy :)
